Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

What industries do you specialize in? +

We work with clients across various industries including finance, healthcare, e-commerce, education, and government sectors. Our team has experience delivering solutions tailored to specific industry requirements and compliance standards.

What is your typical project timeline? +

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, custom software solutions 3-6 months, and enterprise systems 6+ months. We provide detailed timelines after initial consultation and requirements gathering.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance? +

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages including regular updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and technical support. Our maintenance plans ensure your systems remain secure, up-to-date, and optimized.

We implement security-by-design principles, conduct regular vulnerability assessments, use encryption standards, follow OWASP guidelines, and perform penetration testing. All projects include security audits and compliance checks.

We use modern technologies including React, Angular, Vue.js for frontend; Node.js, Python, Java for backend; AWS, Azure, Google Cloud for infrastructure; and Docker, Kubernetes for deployment. We choose technologies based on project requirements.

We maintain transparent communication through weekly meetings, progress reports, and dedicated communication channels. Clients have access to project management tools and can track progress in real-time.

Yes, we provide comprehensive training sessions, user manuals, documentation, and video tutorials. We ensure your team is fully equipped to use and manage the delivered solutions effectively.
